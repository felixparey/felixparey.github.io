1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal information you disclose to us

In Short: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, but we do not require identifiable information like name or email to use our core Services.

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us *if* you contact us directly (e.g., via email) or express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and Services. We do not require registration or collection of direct identifiers like name or email address to use our mobile application.

Information automatically collected

In Short: Diagnostic and usage data is collected automatically *if* you opt-in via your device settings. This data helps us improve our app.

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Services. This information does not reveal your specific identity (like your name or contact information unless you provide it separately, e.g., via email) but includes device, diagnostic, and usage information.

Specifically, if you choose to enable sharing of diagnostic and usage data through your mobile device's operating system settings (e.g., Apple's "Share iPhone & Watch Analytics" or similar options), we may receive information related to your use of our application(s). This data collection is facilitated by the platform provider (e.g., Apple) based on your consent given through the device settings. The information we may receive includes:

  • Log and Usage Data: Service-related, diagnostic, usage, and performance information. This can include details about your activity in the Services (e.g., session duration, features used, performance metrics, date/time stamps), device event information (system activity, error reports/crash dumps, hardware settings), IP address (which may be anonymized or truncated by the platform provider), device identifiers, operating system, and browser/device characteristics.
  • Device Data: Information about the device you use, such as device model, operating system version, device identifiers, device settings, application identification numbers, and potentially mobile carrier or network information associated with crash reports.

This diagnostic and usage information is primarily needed to maintain the security and operation of our Services, to identify and fix bugs, understand usage patterns, improve app performance and features, and for internal analytics and reporting purposes.

All personal information that you *voluntarily* provide to us (e.g., if you contact us via email) must be true, complete, and accurate, and you must notify us of any changes to such personal information.

Sensitive Information

We do not process sensitive personal information.

7. CONTROLS FOR DO-NOT-TRACK FEATURES

Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems and mobile applications include a Do-Not-Track ("DNT") feature or setting you can activate to signal your privacy preference not to have data about your online Browse activities monitored and collected. At this stage, no uniform technology standard for recognizing and implementing DNT signals has been finalized. As such, we do not currently respond to DNT browser signals or any other mechanism that automatically communicates your choice not to be tracked online. If a standard for online tracking is adopted that we must follow in the future, we will inform you about that practice in a revised version of this Privacy Notice.
